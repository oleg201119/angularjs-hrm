Our History

Australia is both renowned and criticised for having one of the most complex and dynamic workplace relations systems in the world. Highly political in nature, the Industrial relations landscape often changes with each consecutive government and as many HR professionals agree, industrial relations is a political football ground.

Over the past 20 years, we at HRM have been working in the fields of HR, ER and Employment Law. Having providing specialist training on behalf of the Fair Work Ombudsman and WorkCover/Worksafe, we have educated hundreds of businesses and senior managers in various aspects of HR compliance. 

Having also won various awards in the process, HRM has been designed to be a platform enabling not only compliance, but also a forum allowing greater communications between HR colleagues.

What We Offer

Compliance and practicable HR tools at a minimal cost. At HRM, we understand the employee life cycle in a workplace. We understand many business cannot afford dedicated and qualified HR professionals and businesses with HR professionals, are often overwhelmed with keeping up with compliance whilst juggling the competing priorities from an operational and sales perspective.

HRM is designed to supplement HR professional’s tools and give reporting system that provide quality metrical data and compliance measures. It is designed in plain English allowing the HR manager to outsource many functions to operational managers while HR coordinating HR operations from a remote location. 

HRM has complex permissions, dashboards and systems allowing HR functions to be managed by key operational managers whilst retaining confidentiality of data.
